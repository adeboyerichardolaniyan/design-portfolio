## Design Language

The visual language for Meridian was built on principles of clarity and composability. Every token, every component, every pattern was designed to work independently and in concert.

## Token Architecture

We developed a three-tier token system: primitive values, semantic tokens, and component-specific tokens. This allowed teams to customize their products while maintaining system-wide consistency.
